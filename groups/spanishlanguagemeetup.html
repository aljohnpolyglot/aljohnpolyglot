This meetup group is for anyone interested in practicing their Spanish, from beginners to advanced speakers. We gather regularly to chat, play games, and enjoy the company of fellow Hispanophiles. It's a fantastic alternative, especially since the large "Spanish for Filipinos" Luneta meetups are temporarily suspended.

Our sessions are designed to be inclusive. If you're new to Spanish, feel free to speak in English, and our members (including native speakers from Colombia, Mexico, and Spain at times!) will gladly help you. We're all here to learn and support each other.
