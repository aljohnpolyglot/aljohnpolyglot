About This Group

Spanish for Filipinos, founded by Eric Martinez, is a dynamic community created for Filipinos learning Spanish and native Spanish-speakers looking to connect. All activities are free and voluntary.

Our core mission is to spark curiosity and encourage fellow Pinoys in their Spanish language journey, fostering connections with Spanish-speakers worldwide. Language schools and independent Spanish teachers are also welcome to share their course offerings. We aim to build a supportive and engaging space for everyone passionate about the Spanish language and Hispanic culture in the Philippines.

Group Rules & Regulations

Prohibited Posts/Comments

  • Politics, Religion, Gender/Sex
  • Offensive posts or comments
  • Unnecessary Conflict/Debates
  • History (Colonialism/Imperialism debates are NOT ALLOWED. Hispanic Heritage/Language history is okay.)
  • Racism, Discrimination/Bullying
  • Spamming
  • Advertisements not related to Spanish Language learning

Allowed Posts

  • Inoffensive Spanish related memes/jokes
  • Photos/Videos of Spanish learning and teaching
  • Questions about grammar, conjugation, etc.
  • Advice for learning Spanish
  • Helpful articles, books, PDFs
  • Travel photos/videos
  • Food related posts
  • Spanish related series, movies, music, etc.

NOTE: We aim to focus on our main objective: to teach and learn Spanish. Polite debate on the SPANISH LANGUAGE itself is allowed, but ad hominems or personal attacks will result in an immediate ban. We welcome all Spanish speakers (Tuteo, Voseo, etc.). Let's maintain a peaceful and enjoyable environment for aprendizaje del Español.
