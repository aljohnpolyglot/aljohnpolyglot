Our Mission: Mentorship & Community

The Club de Hispano-Hablantes Filipino is dedicated to supporting Filipinos in their Spanish learning journey. Our goal is simple: to serve Spanish learners through mentorship.

We foster a collaborative environment where students of all levels can practice, resolve doubts with the help of more experienced speakers, and grow in their confidence and language skills. We firmly believe in the value of community to keep the Hispanic heritage alive and flourishing in the Philippines.

A vibrant and supportive community.

What We Do

Dedicated Mentorship

We facilitate connections between learners and more experienced mentors for personalized and effective guidance.

Lively Practice

We create opportunities for real conversational practice, helping to improve fluency and pronunciation.

Shared Resources

We offer and recommend study materials, digital tools, and practical tips for learning.

United Community

A safe and friendly space to ask questions, share achievements, and celebrate Hispano-Filipino culture.

Signature Event: Tertulia Rizal

Honoring José Rizal

The "Tertulia Rizal" is one of our key events, paying homage to our national hero, José Rizal, a prominent figure in Hispano-Filipino literature and history. These gatherings celebrate his works and the enduring legacy of Spanish in the Philippines.

Event Activities Often Include:

  • Essay Contest (Concurso de Redacción)
  • Readings of Noli Me Tángere
  • Recitation of Rizal's Poems
  • Icebreakers (Rompehielos)
  • Announcement of Contest Winners (with prizes like scholarships, cash)
  • Shared Meal / Merienda Cena

Event Example: December 14, 2024, 2:00 - 5:00 PM at The Gallery, Rizal Park, Luneta.
