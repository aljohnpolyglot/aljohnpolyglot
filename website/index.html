Language & Culture Centers

Not a fan of intense self-study, or looking for structured classes and official certifications? These renowned cultural institutes in the Philippines offer excellent programs, events, and a chance to connect with native speakers and fellow learners. I've collaborated with some, interned at others (like the Philippine Italian Association!), and regularly visit them – you might even see me there!

Instituto Cervantes Manila

Promotes Spanish language and Hispanic culture with courses (DELE exams), cultural events like film festivals, and libraries. You'll often find me at their 'Día del Libro' celebrations and film screenings – check out our video collaboration!

Website

Alliance Française de Manille

A historic institution offering French courses (DELF/DALF) and a rich calendar of cultural events. Perfect for a deep dive into French culture! I practically live in their library on weekends.

Website

Alliance Française de Cebu

Bringing French language and culture to the Visayas with classes and engaging events. A fantastic resource if you're in Cebu! We had a great time filming there – watch the video!

Website

Philippine Italian Association

Promoting Italian culture since 1962! They host incredible events like Cine Europa and offer vital community services through Patronato. I had a fantastic internship experience here and we're planning a video feature soon!

Website

Our video with PIA is on its way! Stay tuned.

Societa Dante Alighieri Manila

Dive into Italian with SDA Manila! Offering comprehensive language courses (A1-C2), translation services, and vibrant cultural events. We've collaborated on some great videos – check one out!

Website

KBRI Manila - Kelas BIPA

Learn Bahasa Indonesia directly from the Indonesian Embassy! They offer fantastic courses and host amazing cultural events that are often featured on my channel. Watch our video from one of their lively gatherings!

Website

Goethe-Institut Philippinen

The official German cultural institute in the Philippines. Perfect for German courses (Goethe-Zertifikat exams), and a diverse range of engaging cultural programs and film screenings.

Website

Future collaborations with Goethe-Institut planned! Watch this space.
