Aljohn's Bookshelf

A curated collection of books that have fueled my curiosity, expanded my understanding of languages, science, and the world, or simply provided great stories in my target languages.

Language Learning & Linguistics

Foundational texts and insightful reads on how we acquire and learn languages.

Principles and Practice in Second Language Acquisition

Stephen Krashen

This book was a game-changer, solidifying my understanding of comprehensible input. Krashen's theories are central to my 'acquisition, not learning' philosophy.

Goodreads Amazon

Fluent Forever

Gabriel Wyner

Wyner's approach to using spaced repetition (like Anki) and creating effective flashcards deeply influenced my memorization techniques.

Goodreads Amazon

Science, Philosophy & Big Ideas

Books that sparked my insatiable curiosity long before my polyglot journey began.

Cosmos

Carl Sagan

Sagan's ability to explain complex cosmic concepts with such wonder was incredibly inspiring. This book fueled my early love for learning.

Goodreads Amazon

A Brief History of Time

Stephen Hawking

Tackling Hawking's ideas pushed my understanding. It showed me the vastness of knowledge yet to be explored.

Goodreads Amazon

Fiction in Target Languages

Reading familiar stories or engaging novels in my target languages was key for immersion.

Harry Potter y la Piedra Filosofal

J.K. Rowling

My first full book in Spanish! Knowing the story allowed me to focus on the language. It took a month and a half, but was a huge milestone.
